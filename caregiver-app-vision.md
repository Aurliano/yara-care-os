Caregiver App Vision

Version: 1.0
Status: Approved
Product: Yara Care
Company: SAYDA Technology

Purpose

Caregiver App is the primary management application of the Yara ecosystem.

Its mission is to help families care for elderly loved ones remotely while preserving the elder's independence, dignity, and quality of life.

The application should make remote care feel natural, reliable and effortless.

Mission

Reduce the anxiety of family members by giving them timely insight into the elder's wellbeing and enabling them to provide support from anywhere.

Vision

Yara is not a medication reminder.

Yara is not a smart tablet.

Yara is not a video calling application.

Yara is a complete elderly care platform.

Product Philosophy

Every feature must satisfy at least one of these goals:

Reduce caregiver anxiety.
Increase elder independence.
Simplify communication.
Improve care quality.
Reduce caregiving costs.

Otherwise, the feature does not belong in Yara.

Product Principles
Elder First

Technology must adapt to the elder.

Never the opposite.

Simplicity First

The caregiver should understand the elder's condition in less than twenty seconds.

Trust Before Intelligence

Reliable systems are always more valuable than clever systems.

Privacy by Design

Respecting the elder's dignity is mandatory.

Monitoring features must always be transparent and configurable.

Collaboration

Caregiving is a collaborative activity.

The platform must support multiple caregivers working together.

Platform Thinking

Every feature should strengthen the Yara ecosystem instead of becoming an isolated function.
