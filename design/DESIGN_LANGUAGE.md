# Design Language

**Product:** Yara Smart Care  
**Module:** Android Hub  
**Version:** MVP v1

---

# Mission

Yara is not designed to impress users.

Yara is designed to comfort them.

Every screen should reduce stress, increase confidence, and make technology disappear.

---

# Product Personality

Yara is:

- Calm
- Respectful
- Trustworthy
- Friendly
- Patient

Yara is NOT:

- Robotic
- Childish
- Cold
- Loud
- Overly playful

---

# Emotional Goal

The elderly user should feel:

"I know how to use this."

instead of

"I'm afraid of touching something."

---

# Design Principles

Simple over Feature-rich

Calm over Exciting

Reliable over Clever

Accessible over Beautiful

Human over Robotic

Technology should stay invisible.

Comfort should stay visible.

---

# Visual Language

The interface should feel:

- Soft
- Clean
- Bright
- Quiet

Avoid visual noise.

Avoid decorative elements.

Avoid unnecessary animations.

Every element must have a purpose.

---

# Color Philosophy

Primary emotion:

Calm.

Recommended palette:

- Warm White
- Soft Gray
- Gentle Green

Accent colors only communicate meaning.

Green

Success

Orange

Reminder

Red

Emergency

Blue

Information

Colors should never become decoration.

---

# Typography Philosophy

Readable before beautiful.

Large text.

High contrast.

Comfortable line spacing.

Numbers must be immediately readable.

---

# Iconography

Rounded.

Simple.

Filled icons preferred.

Icons must always be recognizable by elderly users.

Avoid abstract symbols.

---

# Motion

Motion is functional.

Never decorative.

Preferred transitions:

- Fade
- Small Scale
- Short Duration

Maximum duration:

200ms

---

# Accessibility

Minimum touch target:

64dp

Large buttons.

High contrast.

No tiny labels.

Never rely on color alone.

Support users with weak eyesight.

Support users with shaky hands.

---

# Navigation Philosophy

The Home screen is the center of the experience.

Users leave Home only to complete a task.

After the task finishes:

Return Home automatically.

No deep navigation.

No complex menus.

---

# Interaction Philosophy

One tap whenever possible.

Never require technical knowledge.

Never expose Android concepts.

Always provide immediate feedback.

Always confirm important actions.

---

# Tone of Voice

Professional.

Warm.

Respectful.

Short sentences.

No technical language.

No medical jargon unless necessary.

Examples:

✓ Time to take your medication.

✓ You have a new voice message.

✓ Your daughter is calling.

Avoid:

✗ Error Code 504

✗ Authentication Failed

✗ Bluetooth Disconnected

Translate technology into human language.

---

# What Yara Is

Yara is not a tablet.

Yara is not a medical device.

Yara is not another Android application.

Yara is a calm, respectful and trusted companion
that helps elderly people live more independently.

---

# Success Criteria

When an elderly user first sees Yara, they should feel:

"I can use this."

without anyone explaining how.
