# Yara

> Smart Care Platform

---

## Vision

Yara is a smart care platform designed to help families remotely monitor and support their loved ones through a dedicated Android Hub, a mobile application, and smart connected devices.

---

## MVP Goal

Validate that families can remotely manage medication reminders and monitor the basic safety status of their loved ones.

---

## Target Users

- Elder
- Family Member
- System Administrator

---

## MVP Modules

- Android Hub
- Family App
- Backend API
- Smart Pill Box
- Power Sensor
- Gas Leak Sensor

---

## Out of Scope

The following features are intentionally excluded from the MVP:

- AI Assistant
- Camera
- Fall Detection
- Voice Assistant
- Smart Watch
- Medical Devices
- Home Automation
- OTA Updates

---

## Product Principles

1. Offline First
2. Security by Design
3. Simplicity over Complexity

---

## Success Criteria

The MVP is considered successful if:

- A family member can register an elder.
- Medication reminders are delivered on time.
- Pill box events are synchronized.
- Power and gas alerts are received.
- Device status is visible in the Family App.

---

## Golden Rule

If a feature delays the MVP without significantly increasing user value, it belongs to a future release.
